What I do

Engineering, analytics, and storytelling — all aimed at making decisions and stories clearer.

CRM Analytics & Dashboards

I design Salesforce CRM Analytics dashboards and quote flows that cut noise and surface the right signals for Sales, Finance, and RevOps.

  • Production org examples, not demo sandboxes
  • KPIs tied to real business questions
  • SAQL, dataflows, & UX tuned for speed

Films & YouTube
