From LNCT to Bangalore, Salesforce & films — in 5 phases.

Slide through the journey — each stop is a chapter of how a small-town kid turned code, camera, and chaos into a career.

  1. LNCT, Bhopal – Code + camera

    Started as a CSE student, got equally obsessed with editing, storytelling, and making small experiments with the camera.

  2. Degree mil gaya, offer letter nahi

    Campus placements didn’t click. Everyone had job stories; I had “ab kya karein?” and a head full of unfinished plans.

  3. Imswarnil channel: confusion → content

    Turned the “stuck” phase into story. Started uploading, exploring YouTube, and documenting the gap between small-town life and big-city dreams.

  4. Mahroni to Bangalore, then first dev job

    Moved to Bangalore, survived PG life, walk-ins and rejections, and finally cracked that first startup role while still filming the grind.

  5. CRM Analytics, storytelling & 1M dream

    Shifted into Salesforce / CRM Analytics, building dashboards by day and cinematic stories by night — on a long mission to 1M subscribers.
